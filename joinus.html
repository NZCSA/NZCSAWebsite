Fundraising Manager

Position Overview

Starting in 1996, The New Zealand Chinese Students’ Association (NZCSA) began its life as a student initiative to connect the Chinese students in New Zealand who shared the same vision, providing them with a chance to expand their capabilities as well as associate with like-minded young leaders.

Since then, NZCSA has gone from strength to strength becoming the largest Chinese student organisation while establishing itself as a reputable entity in the local community. NZCSA sees around 1000 new members each year and currently has a committee of 50 students.



About This Position

We are looking for someone to join the NZCSA team as a Fundraising Manager for the year 2016. You will be working alongside like-minded students from the whole Committee to tackle problems in different areas.

As a Fundraising Manager You will have the opportunity to learn and get first-hand experience on:


  • Running the fundraising of Non-Profit or Profit events.
  • Communicate with other business or companies and looking for the valuable sponsors and promote NZCSA.
  • Assist the Director of BD and Associate Director if BD.
  • Help and lead the Fundraising team.
  • Communicating with the other departments in NZCSA.



The Ideal Person

We are looking for someone who is as passionate about making a change as we are. As a Fundraising Manager and a student, we don’t expect you to know everything, but we do expect that you have the desire and the ability to learn.


These additional qualities will just be a bonus:


  • The ability to work coherently in a team
  • Great initiative – always learning and on the lookout for better ways to do things
  • Excellent communication and writing skills in English and Chinese
  • Experience in organisational capacity
  • Experience in research



What we offer


  • A platform to show your skills to the world
  • Practical experience in a broad range of skills sets
  • All the necessary training and guidance
  • A very friendly and open organisational culture
  • Flexible work options
  • A chance to shape the student and wider community


If you are looking to change yourself and these around you, this is a position that will give you plenty of exposure and experience needed for anything you want to do after University.


We have big plans for this year, and are actively seeking a talented and passionate team to help this effort and grow with the organisation.
