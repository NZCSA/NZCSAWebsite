Director of Business Development

Position Overview

About NZCSA



Starting in 1996, The New Zealand Chinese Students’ Association (NZCSA) began its life as a student initiative to connect the Chinese students in New Zealand who shared the same vision, providing them with a chance to expand their capabilities as well as associate with like-minded young leaders.

Since then, NZCSA has gone from strength to strength becoming the largest Chinese student organisation while establishing itself as a reputable entity in the local community. NZCSA sees around 1000 new members each year and currently has a committee of 50 students.



About This Position

We are looking for someone to join the NZCSA team as the Director of the Department of Business Development for the year 2017. You will be working alongside like-minded students from the whole Committee to tackle problems in different areas.

As the Director of the Department of Business Development You will have the opportunity to learn and get first-hand experience on:


  • Making executive decisions for the largest Chinese student organisation in New Zealand.
  • Leading a team.
  • Expanding and maintaining the business, sponsorship and partnership opportunities of NZCSA.
  • Interacting with all types of stakeholders.
  • Be in charge of writing business proposals and doing presentations to stakeholders.



The Ideal Person

We are looking for someone who is as passionate about making a change as we are. As the Director of the Department of Business Development and a student, we don’t expect you to know everything, but we do expect that you have the desire and the ability to learn.


These additional qualities will just be a bonus:


  • The ability to work coherently in a team
  • Great initiative – always learning and on the lookout for better ways to do things
  • Excellent communication and writing skills in English and Chinese
  • Ability to tackle problems individually
  • Leadership
  • Having passion for the NZCSA vision and mission



What we offer


  • A platform to show your skills to the world
  • Practical experience in a broad range of skills sets
  • All the necessary training and guidance
  • A very friendly and open organisational culture
  • Flexible work options
  • A chance to shape the student and wider community
